Remember Vim folding

This is to help me remember the folding commands in Vim:

Keyboard Operation
zd Delete one fold
zD Delete one fold recursivley
zE Eliminate all folds in the window
zo Open one fold
zO Open all folds recursivley
zc Close fold
zC Close all folds recursivley
za Toggle open/closed
zA Recursivley toggle open/closed
zR Open all folds
zM Close all folds
[z Move to the start of current open fold
]z Move to end of current fold
zj Move downwards to start of next fold
zk Move upwards to the end of the previous fold
